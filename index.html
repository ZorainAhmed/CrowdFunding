Mastercraft Bamboo Monitor Riser

A beautiful & handcrafted monitor stand to reduce neck and eye strain.

About this project

The Mastercraft Bamboo Monitor Riser is a sturdy and stylish platform that elevates your screen to a more comfortable viewing height. Placing your monitor at eye level has the potential to improve your posture and make you more comfortable while at work, helping you stay focused on thr task at hand.

Featuring artisan craftsmanship, the simplicity of design creates extra desk space below your computer to allow notepads, pens and USB sticks to be stored under the stand.
